Fruit Pop

A Simple, Healthy Dessert!

Most people consider dessert 'unhealthy', but it doesn't have to be that way. This frozen fruit pop recipe offers a sweet treat that is packed with nutrients.

Ingredients

  • Fruit juice
  • Berries or any fruit of your choice

Instructions

  1. Chop fruit into small chunks.
  2. Place fruit chunks into the mold until it is nearly full.
  3. Pour fruit juice into mold until the mold is full.
  4. Insert popsicle stick and freeze for at least 6 hours.
