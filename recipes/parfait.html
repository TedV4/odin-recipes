Yogurt Parfait

A Simple, Healthy Dessert!

Most people consider dessert 'unhealthy', but it doesn't have to be that way. This yogurt parfait recipe offers a sweet treat that is packed with nutrients.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Berries
  • Granola
  • Honey

Instructions

  1. Add 1/2 cup of berries to a jar or bowl.
  2. Place 1/2 cup of yogurt on top of the berries.
  3. Place 1/3 cup of granola on top of the yogurt.
  4. Drizzle honey on top and your dish is complete!
